Basketball


The game of the Baskteball is invented by Dr.James Naismith
in 1891. It is a team sport in which two teams, most commonly of five players each, opposing
one another on a rectangular court, compete withe primary objective of shooting a basketball
through the defender's hoop while preventing the opposing team from shooting thorugh their own
hoop.
